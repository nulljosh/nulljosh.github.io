# GlyNAC Stack - Health Notes

## GlyNAC Stack (NAC + Glycine)

NAC and Glycine together replenish glutathione -- the body's master antioxidant. Strong research from Baylor College of Medicine on reversing aging biomarkers. Relevant for ADHD/autism support.

### NAC (N-Acetyl Cysteine)
- **What it is**: Mucolytic (breaks up mucus), direct precursor to glutathione
- **Benefits**: Lung health, liver detox, brain health (modulates glutamate), anti-inflammatory
- **Mental health**: Evidence for OCD, repetitive behaviors, addiction, irritability
- **Mechanism**: Provides cysteine (the rate-limiting amino acid for glutathione synthesis)

### Glycine
- **What it is**: Simplest amino acid, sweet-tasting powder
- **Benefits**: Sleep quality, collagen synthesis, anti-inflammatory, neuroprotective
- **Role in GlyNAC**: Pairs with NAC to complete glutathione synthesis (glutathione = glutamate + cysteine + glycine)

### The GlyNAC Combo
- **Research**: Baylor College of Medicine studies (Dr. Rajagopal Sekhar) -- reversed multiple aging biomarkers in older adults
- Restored glutathione levels
- Reduced oxidative stress and inflammation
- Improved mitochondrial function, insulin resistance, cognition, muscle strength
- **Key insight**: Supplementing both together is more effective than either alone

### ADHD/Autism Relevance
- Both conditions associated with elevated oxidative stress and lower glutathione
- NAC: benefits for irritability, repetitive behaviors in autism; may help executive function and focus
- NAC modulates glutamate, often dysregulated in ADHD/autism

## Dosage
- **NAC**: 3g/day (split 1.5g morning + 1.5g evening)
- **Glycine**: 6g/day (split doses, or all at night -- helps sleep)
- **Cost**: ~$30/month total

## Tips
- Take NAC on empty stomach for best absorption (or with vitamin C)
- Glycine can be mixed into water or coffee (slightly sweet)
- Start lower and ramp up over a week
- NAC can thin mucus noticeably -- normal
- Especially useful for lung congestion from smoking
