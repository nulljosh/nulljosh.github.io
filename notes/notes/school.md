# UVIC CS Transfer -- Updated Mar 2026

## Deadlines
- Fall 2026: Jan 31 (PASSED)
- May entry: Apr 15
- Jan 2027 entry: Sep 30
- Apply: educationplannerbc.ca or uvic.ca/apply

## Prerequisites
- CSC 110 or 111 (intro CS)
- CSC 115 or 116 (CS fundamentals)
- MATH 100 or 109
- MATH 101

## GPA Requirements
- Min C+ average (3.0/9.0) on most recent 12 units
- No course below C in CS/SENG/MATH
- Competitive avg: 80-85%

## Discovery Studies (21+, alternate entry)
- No formal admission required
- Max 3 units/term, 12 units total
- After 12 units: must apply to full degree
- Does NOT auto-qualify for CS -- still need prereqs + GPA

## Transfer Credits
- bctransferguide.ca for LEC to UVic equivalencies
- Langara 3.0 credits = UVic 1.5 units typically

- CS advisor: cscadvisor2@uvic.ca
