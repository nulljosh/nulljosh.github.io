## DTC (Disability Tax Credit)
- Claim retroactively for up to 10 years of missed filings
- Adults typically get ~$9k/year back (non-refundable, offsets taxes owed -- lower income = smaller refund)
- The $50k figure floating around is inflated by the child disability benefit
- Worth applying -- the retroactive lump sum alone could be significant
