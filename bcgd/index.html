Homeowner Tips

Safety Tips for Your Garage Door

Since 1991, photo eyes are mandatory on all new garage door openers in Canada. If you don't have these safety sensors on your door, your family is at risk of a tragic and avoidable accident.

1

Stop Using a Misaligned Door

If your door goes crooked suddenly, stop it immediately. It is extremely dangerous to move doors up and down when they are crooked because you will damage the component parts further and risk having the entire door collapse onto your car or loved ones.

2

Check for a Broken Spring

If your door won't open, stop trying immediately and look to see if your spring is broken. Identifying a damaged garage door spring may save you money by preventing you from damaging additional parts as you try to force open a heavy broken door.

3

Test the Weight Balance

Disconnect your garage door from the opener and manually lift it. A properly functioning door should be light enough to balance and not fall down when you let go. If it feels excessively heavy or won't stay put, the springs need professional adjustment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free safety check?
Yes. Every service visit includes a free safety check at no extra charge. We inspect all major components -- springs, cables, rollers, hinges, tracks, and opener safety features -- to make sure your door is operating safely. There's no charge unless you choose to move forward with the repair.
Why don't you install new doors?
We focus 100% on repairs so we can provide honest service without any sales pressure. When you call us, you know we're there to fix your door -- not to sell you a new one. If your door can be fixed, we will make it work like new again. This is a deliberate choice that lets us be the best at what we do.
How long do repairs usually take?
Most repairs are completed in under 90 minutes. We carry common parts on the truck so there's rarely a need for a follow-up visit, without waiting for backorders. From the time we arrive, you'll have a working garage door before you know it.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, we are fully licensed and insured throughout British Columbia. Your home and property are protected. We carry comprehensive liability insurance for every job.
What brands do you work with?
We are LiftMaster specialists. We service all major garage door brands, but our primary expertise and replacement parts are LiftMaster. Our repairs are fully compatible with all LiftMaster systems. We carry LiftMaster openers, remotes, keypads, and accessories on the truck.
Do you offer emergency service?
Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency repair service. If your door is stuck, off-track, or a spring or cable has broken, call us anytime -- day or night. We understand a non-functional garage door is both an inconvenience and a security concern.
What areas do you serve?
We serve the entire Lower Mainland of British Columbia, from North Vancouver and West Vancouver all the way east to Abbotsford and Mission. Our shop is based in Langley, and we have the whole region covered.
