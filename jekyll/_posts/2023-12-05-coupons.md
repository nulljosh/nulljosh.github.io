# Time
Invest in selling expertise or assisting others in what you know.
Focus on gaining attention and sharing valuable information to make the most of your time.
Build trust by demonstrating your unique knowledge and skills to influence others' decisions.
# Information
Sell your expertise, like consulting or assisting others with what you know.
Focus on capturing attention and sharing valuable information.
Establish trust by showcasing your unique knowledge and skills to persuade others to make a purchase.
# Product
Most businesses follow conventional practices.
Mega businesses primarily profit through established methods.
Modern challenges stem from monopolies and the impact of globalism, exemplified by Amazon, where easier access to Chinese knockoffs affects consumer choices. 
# Service
Free up your time by having others invest their time.
Prioritize promoting people over products or services.
Transform human beings into the commodity or offering.
Acknowledge this fundamental aspect of the world's functioning.
