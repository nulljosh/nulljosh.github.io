Echo Privacy Policy

On-device transcription. Last updated May 30, 2026.

Echo collects nothing. There is no account, no server, no analytics, and no tracking. Every transcription runs entirely on your device and never leaves it.

What Echo does with your audio

Echo transcribes speech using a Whisper model that runs locally on your iPhone or Mac. Audio you record or import is processed on-device and is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared. Transcripts are stored only in Echo's local storage on your device.

Data we collect

None. Echo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party trackers. We never see your audio, your transcripts, or how you use the app.

Permissions

  • Microphone is used only to capture audio for live transcription, at the moment you press record. Echo does not listen in the background.
  • File access is used only for audio files you explicitly choose to import.

Purchases

Echo Pro is a one-time, non-consumable unlock handled entirely by Apple's App Store. Echo never sees your payment details. Ownership is verified locally through StoreKit; no purchase data is sent to us, because there is no "us" to send it to.

Children

Echo does not collect data from anyone, including children, and contains no ads or external links to unverified content.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date. Since Echo collects nothing, material changes are unlikely.
